About

Where physics-based simulation meets data-driven discovery

I am a computational materials scientist with over a decade of experience developing multiscale, multiphysics models for nuclear fuels, advanced manufacturing, and components operating in extreme thermomechanical, irradiation, and corrosive environments. My work translates high-fidelity simulation into design-actionable insight across the MOOSE, BISON, and MARMOT frameworks.

Increasingly, I bridge that physics foundation with scientific machine learning and agentic AI — building LLM-driven workflows that expose established simulation codes as callable tools through the Model Context Protocol, so that materials qualification and lifetime prediction can be accelerated through intelligent, automated orchestration.

Research Focus

Core competencies

A rare intersection of nuclear materials domain expertise, computational mechanics, and modern AI systems.

Multiscale Computational Mechanics

Development and implementation of crystal plasticity finite element, phase-field fracture, and coupled damage models for polycrystalline metals, nuclear fuels, and composites under extreme loading.

Nuclear Materials & Qualification

Modeling radiation-induced segregation, swelling, embrittlement, oxide growth, and TRISO particle failure across MOOSE/BISON/MARMOT — supporting LWR and Generation IV qualification workflows.

Computational Framework Development

Extending high-performance multiphysics simulation tools — MOOSE, BISON, MARMOT, FEniCS, LAMMPS — for coupled mechanical, thermal, and microstructural analysis at scale.

Scientific Machine Learning

Constructing Physics-Informed Neural Networks, Gaussian Process surrogates, Bayesian reduced-order models, and deep-learning pipelines for life prediction, UQ, and high-throughput screening.

Agentic AI Workflows

LLM-driven agents that expose simulation codes as tools via the Model Context Protocol, enabling automated orchestration of multiphysics simulations for accelerated discovery.

Advanced Manufacturing & Microstructure

Data-driven microstructure quantification, unsupervised clustering, and texture analysis for laser powder bed fusion — including patent-pending deep-learning quality assurance.
